Privacy Policy

At PRISM, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use PRISM:

  • Account information (name, email address, password hash)
  • Database connection metadata (host, port, database name — never your actual data)
  • Natural language queries you submit through the app
  • Usage analytics (feature usage, session duration, error logs)
  • Device and browser information for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the PRISM service
  • To generate accurate SQL from your natural language queries
  • To send you product updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve AI model accuracy

Important: PRISM does not store the actual contents of your database. We only process query results temporarily in memory to display them to you. No rows from your database are ever saved on our servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • AI model providers (e.g. OpenAI) solely to process your natural language queries
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, GCP) to host and operate our services
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Query history is retained for 90 days by default and can be cleared at any time from your account settings. You can request full deletion of your data by contacting us.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Right to access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to portability — export your data in a readable format
  • Right to object — opt out of certain processing activities

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

Security Policy

Security is core to everything we build. Here's how we protect your data and connections.

🔐

Encrypted Credentials

All database credentials are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3.

🛡️

Zero Data Storage

Query results are never persisted on our servers. Data flows through and is discarded immediately.

🔑

Auth Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We support 2FA and session timeouts for all accounts.

📋

Audit Logs

All query executions are logged with timestamps for security auditing purposes.

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability in PRISM, please report it responsibly to security@prism.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond within 48 hours.

Cookie Policy

Cookies We Use

We do not use: advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, analytics cookies that identify you personally, or any cookies that share your data with ad networks.
